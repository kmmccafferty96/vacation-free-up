Welcome to Vacation Free UP

Vacation Free UP has partnered with select resorts in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan and Northern Wisconsin to provide free or discounted vacations for residents of Wisconsin, Michigan, and Illinois.

With the Covid-19 epidemic decreasing in the next few months, our goal is to give families a much-needed vacation while supporting resort destinations in the Upper Peninsula of Michigan and Northern Wisconsin. Please take a few minutes and complete the survey.

Upon completion of this survey, you will be notified by email of your free or discounted vacation.   Thank you for your time and supporting the travel industry and local workers while enjoying a great vacation.
